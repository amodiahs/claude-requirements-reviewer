# Requirements Review Report

## Overall Requirements Quality Score

52/100

### Top 3 Drivers of Score Reduction

1. Acceptance criteria missing.
2. Multiple ambiguous terms ("fast", "user-friendly", "scale as needed").
3. Undefined external dependency.

---

## High Severity Findings

### Missing Acceptance Criteria

Evidence:

"Acceptance Criteria: TBD"

Impact:

Requirements cannot be validated or tested.

Recommendation:

Define measurable success criteria for all major requirements.

---

### Undefined External Dependency

Evidence:

"Authentication will be handled by another team."

Impact:

Ownership and delivery timeline are unclear.

Recommendation:

Identify responsible team and define interface expectations.

---

## Ambiguous Language

| Text             | Issue                     |
|------------------|---------------------------|
| should provide   | Non-committal requirement |
| fast             | Not measurable            |
| user-friendly    | Subjective                |
| might be able to | Uncertain functionality   |
| scale as needed  | Undefined expectation     |

---

## Recommendations

1. Define measurable acceptance criteria.
2. Replace vague language with quantifiable requirements.
3. Clarify authentication ownership.
4. Define expected scale.
5. Finalize deferred requirements.
